A little about me

I am a Website Designer and Frontend Engineer from Canada. I enjoy designing beautiful (but functional) websites, building automated solutions for businesses, and am especially excited about building websites in Webflow. I am always trying to learn new technologies or tinkering with a side project.

My work has been featured on Contra, YouTube's "Creator on the Rise" (twice), and I am a Certified Webflow Partner.

I love travelling and have been to 23 countries (and counting), although these days I mostly slow-travel to cities for months at a time. I am a huge fan of spicy food, long through-hikes, and producing/writing music, which I used to do professionally. I'm currently based in Portugal where I live with my partner.

You can check out my work above. To get in touch, drop me a line.

What I've been working on

Currently I am building my design agency sitka.design. We've had our work featured on Contra and are proud of all the success we've had for small-medium sized businesses. All design and development is done by me in Webflow.

From 2019 to late 2024, I worked at an Amazon and TikTok Shop agency, working with Fortune 500 companies to grow their sales on the Amazon platform. I learned a ton about SEO, copywriting, project management, and analytics measurements.

I used to produce music professionally for a number of artists and labels, amassing over +300,000,000 streams. I also teach music production via my YouTube channel which has +45k subscribers and +3m lifetime views.

In what feels like a lifetime ago, I worked as a restaurant manager at a Michelin awarded restaurant and Top 200 Restaurant in the world. I designed the cocktail menu and beverage program.
